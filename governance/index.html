Dijets Council

Dijets Council is an on-chain entity of infrastructure builders that can represent passive stakeholders through vote delegations. The council is comprised of several actors, each represented as an on-chain account for overseeing governance tasks.

DIPs

DIPs are design documents providing concise specifications of the improvement or change to the ecosystem and the rationale behind it. Council member authoring & submitting the DIP is responsible for building consensus with community & other members.

Governance Oversight

As Dijets matures and development velocity safely reduces to general operational levels, its ownership can become increasingly distributed across a broad and diverse group of stakeholders. Dijets Council will oversee the transition process.

Governance Portal

The Governance Portal makes it easier for DJT holders to engage in governance activities including proposal polling and voting with an interface thats simpler and easier to follow.

Inclusive & Dynamic Governance

Dijets Governance was designed to address a wide range of issues, including protocol upgrades, staking rewards community initiatives, and for funding bounties through the treasury management. Lowering entry barriers for submitting proposals enables the model to evolve gracefully overtime & at the behest of those who are most aligned with Dijets long term mission.

Outreach and recruitment for ongoing governance roles from within the community will also ensure that the network is governed inclusively with the majority of the stake always commanding the network and its direction.
